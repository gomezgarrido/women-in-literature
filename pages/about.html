ABOUT US

  

WHAT IS 'WOMEN IN LITERATURE'?

As part of the Code First Girls course we were tasked with creating our own website and decided to focus on female authors. We made this decision because women are heavily underrepresented in literature, with studies showing women's books being priced 45% lower than mens. Due to this we wanted to create a space to celebrate all the inspiring women in literature, both past and present. We hope this website encourages other women and young girls to read more and hope you discover your new favourite book or author!
